Where We Came From

Bountiful Foods was founded by John Yellow. Mr. Yellow graduated High School at the age of seven, received his bachelor's degree in Making Good Health Companies, and invented a cure for ebola in 2021. Having conquered every obstacle in life, he set out to overcome the one trial he had yet to face. Nutrition.

John Yellow resolved himself to create the greatest smoothie company the world had ever seen. Thus, Bountiful Foods was born. Bountiful's Foods Specialty Drinks are so incredibly packed with vitamins and minerals, they are said to cure blindness, cleanse your kidneys, and even get you a girlfriend.

John Yellow is currently living in Carlsbad, California with his family.
